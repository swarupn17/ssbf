HomeCity Tour

Explore, Connect, and Learn

The city tour is more than just sightseeing; it's an integrated experience designed to foster spiritual renewal, historical appreciation, and invaluable professional connections within the vibrant setting of Prayagraj.

Discover Prayagraj

A city tour of Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh’s spiritual heart, blends divinity and history. Explore sacred sites, Mughal masterpieces, and historic homes reflecting India’s freedom struggle on a soulful journey through culture and heritage.

Networking Opportunities

The 9th School on Behavioural Finance offers exceptional networking opportunities with experts. Engage with thought leaders during panels, social events, and collaborative sessions to build lasting professional relationships.

Who Should Attend

The programme is open to students, research scholars, academicians, professionals, executives, and entrepreneurs who have an interest in learning behavioural finance. Prior financial knowledge is not a prerequisite.
