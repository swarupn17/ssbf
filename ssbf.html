HomeSchool on Behavioural Finance

What You Will Learn

  • 1

    Introduction to Behavioural Finance

    History, theories, and debates surrounding the rationality of studying Behavioural Finance.

  • 2

    Basic Foundations

    Exploring the core building blocks, including limits to arbitrage and the psychology of decision-making.

  • 3

    Investor Psychology

    Understanding common investor behaviors like naive diversification, excessive trading, and biased decisions.

  • 4

    Prospect Theory

    Studying the Prospect Theory model, its applications, limits, and relevance in modern finance.

  • 5

    Regret Theory

    Analyzing the fear of regret in financial decisions, anticipatory regret, and strategies to minimize it.

  • 6

    And much more...

    Covering mental accounting, overconfidence, anchoring, herd behaviour, and various cognitive biases in finance.

Key Takeaways

This course introduces the fundamentals of behavioural finance, empowering participants to apply this knowledge in everyday decision-making.

Past Schools

The ‘School on Behavioural Finance’ is an unrivalled programme started by IIIT Allahabad in 2018 to encourage research in this novel field. Since its inception, it has attracted a large number of students, researchers, and faculties from across the globe.

The recent pandemic didn’t stop us; the programme saw an upsurge in applications, with over 170 participants in 2020 and 184 in 2021.
