Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad

Established in 1999, the Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad (IIIT-A) is a center of excellence in Information Technology, conferred with the status of an “Institute of National Importance.” IIIT-A aims to develop professional expertise and skilled manpower in IT and related areas, strengthening India's indigenous capabilities to emerge as a leading player in the global arena.

The beautiful 100-acre residential campus, designed on the Penrose Geometry pattern, provides a stimulating environment with all academic and residential areas connected to the Institute network.

About the Department of Management Studies

The Department of Management Studies at IIIT Allahabad was founded with the philosophy of integrating Management and Technology. It propels academic excellence through innovative pedagogy, focusing on creativity, knowledge exchange, and the empowerment of both students and faculty. The department offers a 2-Year Full-Time MBA and a Full-Time Dual Degree MBA-Ph.D. Its core strength lies in its distinguished faculty, who bring exposure from various institutes of national importance.
