International Conference on Behavioural Finance (ICBF)

1st – 5th December 2025 | IIIT Allahabad, Prayagraj

Building upon the success of earlier editions of the School on Behavioural Finance, where participants and resource persons from across the globe gathered to share and disseminate knowledge, the 9th edition proudly introduces an International Conference on Behavioural Finance.

In the quest for deeper understanding of behaviours that deviate from classical paradigms of rationality, this conference is dedicated to contemporary developments in behavioural finance. It welcomes theoretical, empirical, case study, interview-based, and experimental works, offering an interdisciplinary platform for scholars, professionals, and students alike.

Conference Tracks

The conference will feature multiple tracks, invited talks, and special sessions covering diverse themes:

  • Track 1: Qualitative Research – Qualitative approaches and applications in behavioural finance.
  • Track 2: Marketing and Behavioural Finance – Consumer financial behaviour, product marketing, digital & green marketing, and emerging intersections of marketing and finance.
  • Track 3: Human Resource and Behavioural Finance – HR practices and their impact on individual and organizational financial decisions.
  • Track 4: Operations Management and Behavioural Finance – Operational aspects shaping financial behaviour and decision-making.
  • Track 5: IT and Systems Management – Technology, IT systems, and digital innovations influencing finance.
  • Track 6: Contemporary Issues in Management – Emerging developments in management, economics, and commerce intersecting with behavioural finance.
  • Track 7: Business Informatics and FinTech – AI analytics, blockchain, digital payments, and fintech innovations reshaping financial services.
  • Special Tracks – Proposals invited on topics such as sustainable marketing, fintech innovations, and AI-driven consumer insights. Submit your proposal to ssbf@iiita.ac.in.

Paper Submission Guidelines

  • Papers must be original and unpublished, aligned with the conference theme.
  • Structured Abstract: 200–250 words (include objectives, methodology, and key findings).
  • Full Paper: 6,000–6,500 words.
  • Suggested structure: Introduction, Review of Literature, Research Gap, Methodology, Findings, Contributions, Limitations, and Scope for Future Research.
  • Formatting: A4, Times New Roman, size 12, 1.5 spacing.
  • Reference style: APA (6th edition).

Best Paper Award

Best Papers will be recognized by the committee under the following categories:

  • Category 1: Academicians, Corporate Professionals, Research Scholars
  • Category 2: Students

Important Dates

  • Abstract Submission Deadline: 31st October 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: 10th November 2025
  • Full Paper Submission: 15th November 2025
  • Conference Registration Deadline: 20th November 2025
