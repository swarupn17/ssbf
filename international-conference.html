International Conference on Behavioural Finance (ICBF)

25th-29th May 2026 | IIIT Allahabad, Prayagraj

Building upon the success of earlier editions of the School on Behavioural Finance, the 9th edition proudly introduces an International Conference dedicated to contemporary developments in the field. We welcome theoretical, empirical, and experimental works, offering an interdisciplinary platform for scholars, professionals, and students.

Conference Tracks

The conference features multiple tracks covering diverse themes:

  • Qualitative Research: Approaches and applications in behavioural finance.
  • Marketing: Consumer financial behaviour, digital & green marketing.
  • Human Resources: HR practices and their impact on financial decisions.
  • Operations Management: Operational aspects shaping financial behaviour.
  • IT and Systems: Technology and digital innovations influencing finance.
  • Contemporary Issues: Emerging developments in management and economics.
  • Business Informatics & FinTech: AI analytics, blockchain, and digital payments.
  • Special Tracks: Proposals on topics like sustainable marketing and AI-driven insights are invited.
